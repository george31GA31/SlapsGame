Master the Art of Slaps

Speed, reflexes, and strategy. Here is everything you need to know to become a champion.

The Objective

The goal is simple: Get rid of ALL your cards. You win the match by playing your cards quciker than your opponent and play your last card.

Higher or Lower

Play cards from your hand to the Center Piles. You can only play a card if it is 1 Rank Higher or 1 Rank Lower than the top card.

If Center is 9: Play 8 or 10. If Center is King: Play Queen or Ace. (Ace wraps to 2!)

The Slap

Keep an eye on the Top Cards of both piles. If they show the Same Rank (e.g., two Jacks), press SPACE immediately!

Winner: The winner forces the LOSER to pick up all cards.

Controls

  • Drag & Drop: Move cards from hand to center.
  • Spacebar: Press to SLAP.

Referees & Penalties

Don't spam! If you slap incorrectly:

Warning +3 Card Penalty
