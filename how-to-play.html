Master the Art of Slaps

Speed, reflexes, and strategy. Here is everything you need to know to become a champion.

The Objective

The goal is simple: Bankrupt your opponent.

You win the match when your opponent runs out of cards completely.

Higher or Lower

Play cards from your hand to the Center Piles.

You can only play a card if it is 1 Rank Higher or 1 Rank Lower than the top card.

  • If Center is 9: Play 8 or 10.
  • If Center is King: Play Queen or Ace.
  • Ace wraps to 2!

Controls

Drag & Drop: Click and drag cards from your hand to the center piles.

Spacebar: Press SPACE to SLAP the pile when a match occurs.

The Slap

Keep an eye on the Top Cards of both center piles.

If they show the Same Rank (e.g., two Jacks, two 7s), press SPACE immediately!

Winner: The winner forces the LOSER to pick up all the cards in the pile (adding to their total).

Referees & Penalties

Don't spam the spacebar! If you slap incorrectly:

  • Yellow Card: Warning for spamming or bad slaps.
  • Red Card: After 2 Yellows. You receive a +3 Card Penalty (You take 3 cards from your opponent).

Rounds vs. Match

Round Over: Happens when one player's hand is empty. Scores update based on total cards remaining.

Match Win: The game continues until one player reaches 0 Cards.
